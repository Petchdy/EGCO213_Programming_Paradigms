This is a game (Guess who is your target?)
The rules are simple.
1. This is a turn-based game. (yours and bot)
2. In your turn you can either ask or guess.
2.1. Ask: check if your target has and doesn't have what you ask for.
2.2. Guess: you can only win the game by guessing the right person.
3. The bot also has 2 choices ask & guess.
4. Make sure you can guess the target correctly before the bot does.
